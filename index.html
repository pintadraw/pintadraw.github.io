Terms of Service

Payment

Full payment is required upfront before work begins. All prices are in USD.

Payment via Lavatop (PayPal or bank card) or EasyStaff Invoice (bank card).
→ Payment guide for EasyStaff

What I will draw

  • Original characters (OC)
  • Fanart — any fandom, games, films, series, books
  • Fantasy of all kinds — light, dark, everything in between
  • Book covers & illustrations
  • Portraits & character art

What I will not draw

  • NSFW / explicit content
  • Hate content of any kind
  • Heavily mechanical subjects (mecha, robots)
  • Real people without their consent

Revisions

3 minor revisions are included free of charge at early stages (sketch / base color). Major changes after sketch approval or revisions beyond the included limit may incur an additional fee. Please provide all references before work begins.

Timeline

Without a set deadline, completion may take up to 90 days depending on complexity and queue. I will keep you updated on progress. If you have a deadline, please let me know before ordering.

Usage rights

You may use the finished artwork for personal use. Commercial use (book covers, merchandise, prints for sale, etc.) requires a separate agreement and additional fee.

The artist retains full copyright and all intellectual property rights to the illustration. I reserve the right to display the finished work in my portfolio, on social media, and in any promotional materials, unless otherwise agreed in writing before work begins.
